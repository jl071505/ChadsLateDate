GAME CONTROLS

These are all the controls you will need to help Chad move around and complete tasks

  • UP ARROW - jump
  • RIGHT ARROW - move right
  • LEFT ARROW - move left
  • SPACE BAR - shoot roses

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT CHAD

Chad is a very messy person. His school life is messy, his love life is messy, and even his ROOM is messy!

Help Chad pick up his items he needs for his date, they're scatterd all over his room and he only has 10 seconds to get ready!
